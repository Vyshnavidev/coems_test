Robust Modules that Speak Volume

COEMS presents itself as a custom-fit solution designed to meet your company's distinct needs

Enquiry

The Enquiry Module transforms emails into inquiries and supports users in handling them as indications after quote submission. Once finalized, these indications seamlessly transition into fixtures.

Fixtures

The Fixture Module provides a detailed specification of a fixed order or cargo. It comes with a checklist and related tasks, assisting charterers in managing fixtures from initiation to delivery.

Voyage Estimate

Accurately calculates all voyage costs, considering the commercials related to the fixtures and indications which are later transformed into live voyages to compare actual and estimated costs.

Operations

The Operation module offers real-time voyage management, providing a detailed view of actual voyage costs and operational statuses. It also offers comprehensive fleet positions visible to all users.

Engineering

A comprehensive system for engineering teams to track and record communications, facilitate task management, time tracking, and reporting.

Projects

This module is designed to handle large-scale, long-term projects that involve multiple fixtures, voyages, and vessels.

Tasks

The task management module efficiently handles tasks related to fixtures, project cargos, and engineering work. It organizes and tracks them effectively while offering thorough worklog reporting.
